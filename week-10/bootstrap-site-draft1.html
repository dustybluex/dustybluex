Gum

Gum, the existence of which is often ignored by us, is actually very important. It supports the teeth, nd in many cases, it is the source for dental diseases. Themed with gum, i designed many accessories and shot many pictures of the actual gums in some interesting and odd methods. When I wore retainers, I noticed the flesh pink self-curing resin used by the dentist. The color and quality of the self-curing resin were suitable for my design, and with the application of this material, the accessories I have designed were quite natural and looked exactly like the real gum.
